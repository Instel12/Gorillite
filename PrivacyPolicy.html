Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Gorillite VR collects, uses, and discloses personal information when you use our virtual reality game.

We don't collect any personal information from you when you use our game, however, our multiplayer services (Photon Engine and PlayFab) collect the following information:

  • Username
  • IP address
  • Device ID

We may view this data under the following circumstances:

  • User requests for support or account management
  • If you're reported breaking the Terms of Service
  • While unlikely, we may view this data to comply with legal obligations

Gorillite VR doesn't share any of the data we view outside of the development team. Photon Engine and PlayFab are the only third parties that may have access to this data.
